Giulio Venneri: The second youngest member is the E-guitar player of the Drones. Born in Italy, the Boss writes the epic songs that the Drones 72 play.

Drønes'72 is a Rock band consisting of four talented members, Francesco Morini (Bass Guitar), Wolfgang 'the wolf' Schlaeger (Drums and Percussions), Franceso Tramontin (Vocals and Acoustic Guitar) and Giulio Venneri (E-Guitar and Vocals). Since their first live concert on the 6th of March 2016, the Drønes'72 have been rocking around Europe, but mostly Belgium.

Wolfgang Schlaeger:The Wolf was born in Germany and is the oldest band member. His drumming skills are said to be unbeatable and is the Drones'72 's number 1 drummer. He keeps the beat.

Francesco Tramontin: The singer and acoustic guitar player was born in Italy and is the second oldest member. His perfect singing voice makes the music sound twice as good as a lot of other bands.

Francesco Morini:The youngest member and Bass guitar player. His young age may prove that he hasn't been practicing for as long as older bass players; but his skills make that very hard to tell.
